Impact X Framework

The Impact X Framework is G2i's strategic methodology for measuring and maximizing business impact. Understanding this framework demonstrates alignment with company values.

What is Impact X?

Impact X is a holistic approach to evaluating projects and initiatives based on their real-world impact, scalability, and alignment with company mission. It goes beyond traditional metrics to include stakeholder value, market reach, and long-term sustainability.

The Five Pillars of Impact X

1. Relevance

Does the initiative address a genuine market need?
Is it aligned with company strategic goals?
Does it serve our target customer segments?

2. Reach

How many users or customers will be affected?
What's the geographic or segment expansion potential?
Can we scale this initiative across markets?

3. Resilience

Is the solution sustainable long-term?
How does it perform under market stress?
What's the risk mitigation strategy?

4. Return

What's the financial impact (revenue, cost savings)?
What's the opportunity cost?
How do we measure ROI?

5. Resonance

Does it align with company values and culture?
Does it inspire and engage the team?
What's the stakeholder sentiment?

Applying Impact X in Your Role

When proposing initiatives or evaluating opportunities, use Impact X as your framework. This demonstrates strategic thinking and alignment with G2i's decision-making culture.

NaGringa Framework

NaGringa is G2i's customer success and relationship management framework, emphasizing cultural adaptation, growth mindset, and authentic engagement.

Understanding NaGringa

The term "NaGringa" reflects G2i's global mindset. It represents the ability to work authentically across cultures while maintaining strong relationships and driving mutual success.

Core Philosophy:

Success comes from genuine understanding of customer needs, cultural sensitivity, and creating win-win partnerships that drive long-term value.

Six Dimensions of NaGringa

N - Nurturance

Building genuine relationships based on trust and mutual benefit. Investing in customer success beyond transactional interactions.

A - Adaptability

Understanding cultural differences and adjusting communication styles. Respecting diverse work practices and preferences.

G - Growth

Supporting customer business growth. Creating opportunities for mutual expansion and scaling together.

R - Reliability

Delivering consistent quality and meeting commitments. Being a dependable partner through ups and downs.

I - Innovation

Bringing creative solutions and best practices. Continuously improving how we serve and create value.

N - Network

Leveraging the G2i community and ecosystem. Connecting customers with resources, expertise, and opportunities.

NaGringa in Practice

  • Regular check-ins beyond project scope
  • Understanding customer business strategy
  • Proactive communication about changes
  • Celebrating customer successes
  • Creating referral and expansion opportunities

RLHF Context & AI Integration

RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback) represents G2i's commitment to AI-driven improvements informed by human expertise and feedback.

What is RLHF?

RLHF combines:
  • Machine learning algorithms that improve over time
  • Human feedback from customers, freelancers, and team members
  • Continuous iteration to optimize platform features and matching

G2i's RLHF Applications

Matching Algorithm

G2i uses RLHF to continuously improve how freelancers are matched with clients. Human feedback on match quality, project success, and satisfaction feeds into algorithm refinement.

Customer Communication

Learning from successful and unsuccessful client interactions to refine messaging, platform prompts, and recommendations.

Quality Assessment

Feedback from both sides of the platform (clients evaluating freelancers, freelancers evaluating clients) helps maintain and improve quality standards.

Feature Development

User feedback directly informs which platform features get developed, tested, and deployed. This human-centered approach ensures builds meet real needs.

Why RLHF Matters for Your Role

  • Data-Driven Culture: G2i values metrics and feedback loops
  • Customer Obsession: Your role will involve gathering and acting on feedback
  • Continuous Improvement: Embracing iteration and learning from failures
  • Human-AI Partnership: Leveraging technology while maintaining human judgment

Example RLHF in Practice

1. Freelancer is matched with Client (algorithm v1) 2. Project completes - Client satisfaction: 4/5 3. Feedback: "Great skills, but communication could be clearer" 4. Algorithm learns: prioritize communicators for this client profile 5. Future matches improve based on feedback

Anticipated Interview Questions

Prepare thoughtful responses to these likely interview questions. Use your stories and frameworks to structure compelling answers.

Background & Experience

Tell me about yourself

Strategy: 2-3 minute narrative covering: background, key accomplishments, why G2i, what excites you about the role.

Why are you interested in G2i?

Strategy: Connect company mission to your values. Mention specific products/initiatives. Show you've done research.

What attracted you to this specific role?

Strategy: Highlight alignment between your skills and role requirements. Show understanding of responsibilities.

Behavioral Questions

Tell me about a time you had to manage a difficult client/stakeholder

Strategy: Use STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result). Show emotional intelligence and problem-solving.

Describe a situation where you had to make a decision with incomplete information

Strategy: Demonstrate decision-making framework and ability to act confidently despite uncertainty.

Give an example of how you've used data to drive a business decision

Strategy: Show analytical thinking and metrics-driven mindset. Include impact of decision.

Technical/Strategic Questions

How would you approach growing our freelancer base in a new market?

Strategy: Break into phases. Consider supply-side and demand-side. Reference Impact X framework.

What do you see as G2i's biggest competitive threats?

Strategy: Show market awareness. Propose solutions. Be balanced (acknowledge strengths too).

How would you measure success in the first 90 days?

Strategy: Set 2-3 metrics focused on impact. Show you understand role and company goals.

Cultural Fit Questions

How do you handle working in ambiguous situations?

Strategy: Show comfort with startup environment. Reference RLHF and continuous learning.

Tell me about your collaboration style

Strategy: Emphasize cross-functional teamwork. Show respect for diverse perspectives. Reference NaGringa values.

Develop 5-7 compelling stories that showcase relevant skills. Use the STAR method for clear, structured storytelling.

Story Framework Template

Story Title: [Your Achievement]

Situation: Context and background. What was the challenge or opportunity?

Task: Your specific responsibility. What did you need to accomplish?

Action: Specific steps you took. What decisions did you make? How did you lead?

Result: Quantifiable outcomes. What was the impact? What did you learn?

Story Categories to Prepare

Story 1: Leadership & Initiative

Example: Leading a team project, taking ownership, driving results despite obstacles.

Story 2: Problem Solving

Example: Identifying a business problem, analyzing data, implementing creative solution.

Story 3: Cross-Functional Collaboration

Example: Working with diverse teams, managing stakeholders, achieving alignment.

Story 4: Customer Impact

Example: Improving customer experience, building relationships, driving retention.

Story 5: Handling Failure/Setback

Example: Learning from mistakes, adapting approach, recovering and succeeding.

Story 6: Continuous Learning

Example: Acquiring new skill, adapting to change, taking on stretch assignment.

Story 7: Startup/Fast-Growth Environment

Example: Thriving in ambiguity, wearing multiple hats, scaling operations.

Story Quality Checklist

  • ✓ Specific and detailed (avoid vague generalities)
  • ✓ Shows your individual contribution (use "I" not "we")
  • ✓ Highlights relevant skills for the role
  • ✓ Includes measurable results or outcomes
  • ✓ Authentic and believable
  • ✓ 2-3 minutes to tell (not rushed, not rambling)
  • ✓ Positive conclusion (what you learned)

Pre-Interview Checklist

Use this checklist in the days and hours before your interview to ensure complete preparation.

One Week Before

  • ☐ Review company mission, vision, and core values
  • ☐ Research leadership team (CEO, your potential manager)
  • ☐ Read recent company blog posts, press releases, and news
  • ☐ Understand the market and competitive positioning
  • ☐ Identify 2-3 thoughtful questions to ask interviewers
  • ☐ Review your relevant work samples or portfolio

Three Days Before

  • ☐ Review and refine your 5-7 STAR stories
  • ☐ Practice saying them aloud (2-3 min each)
  • ☐ Know the name and title of each interviewer
  • ☐ Review the role description carefully
  • ☐ Prepare a 30-second "tell me about yourself" pitch
  • ☐ Know G2i's Impact X and NaGringa frameworks cold

Day Before

  • ☐ Get a good night's sleep (7-8 hours)
  • ☐ Prepare interview outfit (professional, company-appropriate)
  • ☐ Test video/audio if remote interview
  • ☐ Know your interview timeline and format
  • ☐ Have copies of your resume and notepad ready
  • ☐ Review driving directions or transit route if in-person
  • ☐ Do light review of talking points (don't cram)

Interview Day

  • ☐ Eat a healthy breakfast
  • ☐ Arrive 10-15 minutes early (in-person) or 5 minutes early (video)
  • ☐ Dress professionally and confidently
  • ☐ Bring notebook and pen
  • ☐ Have water nearby
  • ☐ Silence your phone
  • ☐ Take deep breaths and calm your nerves
  • ☐ Smile and make eye contact
  • ☐ Speak clearly and confidently
  • ☐ Listen actively to interviewer questions

During the Interview

Key Mindset:

Remember, this is a two-way conversation. You're evaluating G2i as much as they're evaluating you. Be authentic, enthusiastic, and genuine.

Do:
  • ✓ Answer questions directly and concisely
  • ✓ Use examples from your experience
  • ✓ Reference Impact X and NaGringa frameworks where relevant
  • ✓ Ask thoughtful questions about the role and company
  • ✓ Show genuine interest in G2i's mission
  • ✓ Be enthusiastic about the opportunity
  • ✓ Take notes on interviewer answers
Don't:
  • ✗ Interrupt the interviewer
  • ✗ Speak negatively about previous employers
  • ✗ Exaggerate accomplishments or abilities
  • ✗ Ask about salary/benefits in first interview
  • ✗ Check your phone or watch obsessively
  • ✗ Monopolize conversation time
  • ✗ Appear desperate or overeager

After the Interview

  • ☐ Thank your interviewer(s) sincerely
  • ☐ Send thank-you email within 24 hours
  • ☐ Personalize thank-you with specific talking points from interview
  • ☐ Reiterate your interest in the role
  • ☐ Add any additional information that would strengthen your candidacy
  • ☐ Be patient with hiring timeline
  • ☐ Follow up if you don't hear back within agreed timeframe

Success Metrics

  • ✓ Showed understanding of G2i's business, markets, and frameworks
  • ✓ Asked thoughtful questions about the role and company
